Why this webinar

Most AI advice wasn't built for safari work.

Generic tutorials cover the tool. They don't cover your enquiries, your tone, your admin, or the specific work your team does every day.

Repetitive admin is eating your day

Enquiry drafts, note summaries, follow-ups, handovers. The same work repeating every week with no system to speed it up.

AI gives you generic output by default

Without the right setup, AI doesn't know your tone, your destinations, or your guests. Output sounds like it was written for anyone.

No industry playbook exists yet

Most safari operators haven't figured this out. Tutorials online are built for marketers, agencies, or tech teams, not your operation.

Tips aren't team workflows

One clever prompt solves the problem once. You need a repeatable system your whole team uses consistently across sales, ops, and guest experience.

What you will learn

Use your preferred AI tool to make daily safari work faster, clearer, and more consistent.

Summarize messy notes instantly

Turn rough call notes, enquiry emails, and internal handovers into clean summaries your team can act on.

Respond to enquiries faster

Go from a messy guest enquiry to a first reply, follow-up, and proposal starting point without starting from scratch.

Create reusable team workflows

Turn repeated admin tasks into reusable prompts your whole team can use across sales, reservations, and guest experience.

Turn guest questions into content

The questions your team answers every week become useful marketing content your business can reuse.

Set up AI with your safari context

Make your AI tool sound like your team, not a generic assistant. Give it your tone, rules, and real examples.

Keep human judgment in control

AI speeds up the work. You and your team stay in control of every response, decision, and traveler relationship.

Who this is for

Built for the people handling the real work of running a safari business.

Safari Business Owners Founders and owners who want to work smarter across every part of the operation.
Sales & Reservations Teams Anyone handling enquiries, follow-ups, and proposals who wants to move faster without cutting quality.
Guest Experience Teams Teams managing pre-arrival communication, itinerary updates, and on-trip guest support.
Operations Managers People coordinating guides, partners, and logistics who need cleaner internal handovers.
Small Safari Teams Lean teams where one person covers sales, ops, and marketing and AI needs to pull real weight.
Anyone Tired of Generic AI Advice People who have tried AI tools but want workflows built specifically for safari operations, not generic prompts.
